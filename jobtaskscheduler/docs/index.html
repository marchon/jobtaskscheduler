Job Task Scheduler

A very simple scheulder for frappe using rq-schedule

Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for Job Task Scheduler and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app jobtaskscheduler https://github.com/giovanni-codrotech/jobtaskscheduler
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app jobtaskscheduler

Login to your site to configure the app.
